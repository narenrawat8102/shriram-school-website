the sriram millenium school faridabad

What lies at the centre of the mission of The Shriram Millennium School, Faridabad, is a deep commitment to nurture within a safe and supportive environment, the potential of each child in our care, with their individual and unique qualities. The core values of Sensitivity, Integrity, Pride in One’s Own Heritage and Pursuit of Excellence enable our children to contribute responsibly to the environment while implementing innovative ideas and using their leadership skills to impact the community positively. Being an inclusive school, collaborating with peers is an important life-skill taught across classes.

With a highly competent teaching faculty, we help build concepts experientially, helping students to identify and apply them in real life, analyse them and further evaluate what the learning has been. Honing life-skills and supporting the emotional well-being of our children is also an integral part of our curriculum. Sports and a variety of other co-curricular activities form part of a regular day for the children here. Students have excelled in all fields, ranging from academics to sports at the district, state and national level reiterating the core value of Pursuit in Excellence.


The Shriram Millennium School, Faridabad is a K-12 school affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education and offers the CBSE Curriculum. With a sustainable building and green environ, the campus located at Sector 81 offers all the facilities and amenities that can help foster a love for learning.
